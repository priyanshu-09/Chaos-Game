HELLO! WELCOME TO THE CHAOS GAME.

FIRST OF ALL IF YOU'RE VIEWING THIS ON MOBILE, PLEASE SWITCH TO A PC OR JUST ENABLE THE DESKTOP SITE MODE ON YOUR BROWSER.

HOW DOES IT WORK -:
  1. 'N' IS THE NUMBER OF VERTICES OF THE POLYGON TO BE CREATED

  2. A RANDOM POINT WILL BE CREATED IN THE AREA WHICH WILL BE REPRESENTED BY A BIG WHITE DOT.

  3. A VERTEX OF THE POLYGON WILL BE CHOSEN RANDOMLY BASED ON THE PROBABILITIES MENTIONED OF EACH OF THE VERTEX.

  4. LET THERE BE SOME DISTANCE D BETWEEN THE RANDOM POINT AND THE CHOSEN VERTEX, MULTIPLY THIS DISTANCE BY 'R'

  5. TRAVEL 'R TIMES D' FROM THE RANDOM POINT IN THE DIRECTION OF THE CHOSEN VERTEX

  6. GO BACK TO THE THIRD POINT


YOU CAN CHOOSE BETWEEN DIFFERENT SPEEDS OF ANIMATION, IRREGULAR OR REGULAR POLYGON AMONG THE ABOVE MENTIONED VALUES.

YOU CAN ALSO DYNAMICALLY CHANGE 'R' AND PROBABILITIES WHILE RUNNING THE ANIMATION

IF YOU'RE STILL CONFUSED REFER TO THIS YOUTUBE VIDEO.

NOW SIT BACK AND WATCH THE BEAUTIFUL PATTERNS UNFOLD ON YOUR SCREEN AND DON'T FORGET TO FIDDLE AROUND WITH THE VALUES.
